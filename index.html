Faqs

What is a Subcomm?

The full form of "Subcomm" is Subcommittee Meeting. All the GC General Secretaries from different halls of residence have at least one meeting with G.Sec and Secretary TSG before an event for finalizing the dates, choicable slots deciding judges etc. This meeting is called "Subcomm".

Where is Kuntal from?

Kuntal Das is from Chakdaha city (West Bengal), which has population over 1.3 Lakh.

Why did he choose Social and Cultural?

Being a member of Druheen (BTDS), he had taken part in inter hall Hindi Dramatics and Bengali Dramatics in his first year. The management of such a big committee felt such interesting that KD swapped to LBS and became a Secretary.
